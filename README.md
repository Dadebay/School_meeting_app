## Empowering Educators: Seamless Collaboration for School Meetings

**Welcome to Okul_com_tm!**

Okul_com_tm is the ultimate platform that strengthens communication and streamlines meetings for teachers and students. Our platform allows teachers to effortlessly schedule meetings with their classes, ensuring that students are always in the loop.

### Key Features:
- **Planning:** Create and organize meetings with ease.
- **Location:** Specify where the meetings will be held.
- **Teacher:** Identify which teacher is leading the meeting.
- **Classmates:** Ensure that the right students are invited to the meeting.
- **Description:** Provide detailed information about the meeting’s agenda.

### Actions:
- **Reschedule:** Need to change the meeting time? No problem.
  - **Reschedule Lesson:** Quickly reschedule lessons to accommodate everyone's schedule.
- **Select Time and Date:** Conveniently choose the best time and date for your meetings.
- **Send Notifications:** Automatically send notifications to students about upcoming meetings.
- **Cancel:** Cancel meetings if necessary, with ease.
